General Help

Welcome to the Healthcare Management System. Below are some general instructions to help you get started:

  • Patient Management: Manage patient records, view details, and schedule appointments.
  • Provider Management: Add and manage healthcare providers.
  • Appointment Scheduling: Schedule new appointments and view appointment history.
  • Analytics: Access data analytics and insights on patient care and appointments.
Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
To add a new patient, go to the Patient Management section and click on Add New Patient. Fill in the required details and submit the form.
To schedule an appointment, navigate to the Appointment Scheduling section, select the patient and provider, choose a date and time, and submit the form.
